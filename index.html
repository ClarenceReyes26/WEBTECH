Saint Clairmont

Saint Clairmont, also known as Clarence Reyes, is an R&B/alternative artist from the Philippines. Growing up in diverse environments, their sound and style reflect a blend of influences. Like a true multi-creative, Saint Clairmont seamlessly crosses boundaries between music, art, and culture, carving out a unique space in the genre-bending world. Their work exemplifies the spirit of independent artistry, embodying both depth and versatility.
